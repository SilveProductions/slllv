///FAQ

Who are you and what are you doing?

Hey, I'm SLLLV. I'm a character artist, illustrator and game designer. I'm currently working on SunCity lore.

SunCity?

SunCity is a fictional city where all of my original characters and events take place. I plan to expand this universe more and more with time.

What does SLLLV mean?

I used to like silver (the color) so I made up the acronym SLVR. It was pretty obvious and meaningless, so I removed the final letter and multiplied that L, that seemed pretty lonely to me.

Commissions?

Commissions are always open. Feel free to submit any interesting project you have along with your available budget. If your commission has been accepted, I will reach back to you.

Can I use your art? Can I draw your characters?

I'm okay with anything that doesn't involve claiming my art as your own or monetizing my work. Using the hashtag "#slllv" to credit me is greatly appreciated. I am free to make sure I don't get associated with works that depicts values I do not resonate with.
