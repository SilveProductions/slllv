///FAQ

Who are you?

> Hey, I'm SLLLV. I'm a character artist. I design and develop original characters for the animation and streaming industries.

Commissions?

Commissions are usually open. Feel free to submit a detailed description of your character along with your available budget. If your commission has been accepted, I will reach back to you. Make sure to read the terms of services before you commission!

Can I use your art? Can I draw your characters?

I'm okay with anything that doesn't involve claiming my art and designs as your own or monetizing my work. Giving me credit for my work is greatly appreciated and the right thing to do. I am free to make sure I don't get associated with works that depicts values I do not resonate with.
